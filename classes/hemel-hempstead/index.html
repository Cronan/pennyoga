Thursday afternoons · Hemel Hempstead

Gentle Yoga in Hemel Hempstead

A friendly, weekly gentle Hatha Yoga class in central Hemel Hempstead at Carey Baptist Church on Marlowes, opposite the Marlowes Shopping Centre. Thursdays from 2:00pm to 3:15pm, taught by BWY-qualified teacher Penny Cronyn since 2004. £10 per session, booked as a block.

The class

Each session lasts 75 minutes and combines simple breathwork, mindful movement and gentle Hatha Yoga postures, finishing with a guided relaxation. The pace is unhurried and modifications are offered throughout, so the class suits beginners, returners, and people who want a kinder, less strenuous practice. Many regulars have been coming for years; new students are warmly welcomed.

Carey Baptist Church

Carey Baptist Church sits on Marlowes in central Hemel Hempstead, opposite the Marlowes Shopping Centre and a short walk from the bus station and the Old Town. The room used for yoga is warm and quiet, with a wooden floor that suits yoga mats, and chairs are available for any seated work.

Getting there

  • By bus: Hemel Hempstead bus station is a few minutes' walk away; town routes stop along Marlowes itself.
  • By car: short-stay parking is available at the Marlowes Multi-storey Car Park and other town-centre car parks. Please check signage for current rates.
  • By train: roughly 15 minutes by bus or taxi from Hemel Hempstead railway station, or about a 25-minute walk.

What to bring

  • A yoga mat (a few spares are usually available if you forget yours).
  • Loose, comfortable clothing you can move in.
  • A blanket if you have one — useful for the relaxation at the end.

Pricing and booking

Classes are £10 per session and are booked as a block (typically one term at a time). If you are new to the class, Penny offers a one-off trial session so you can try a class before committing. Get in touch by email or phone to arrange a trial.
