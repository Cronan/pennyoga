Tuesday lunchtime & afternoon · Hemel Hempstead

Gentle Yoga at Leverstock Green Village Hall

Two friendly gentle Hatha Yoga classes each Tuesday at Leverstock Green Village Hall, in the village just east of Hemel Hempstead. 12:30pm to 1:45pm and 2:00pm to 3:15pm, taught by BWY-qualified teacher Penny Cronyn since 2004. £10 per session, booked as a block.

The classes

Each session lasts 75 minutes and combines simple breathwork, mindful movement and gentle Hatha Yoga postures, finishing with a guided relaxation. Both groups are warm and welcoming; modifications are offered throughout, so the classes work for beginners, returners and anyone who wants a kinder practice.

Leverstock Green Village Hall

The village hall sits on the green at the heart of Leverstock Green, with the Leverstock Green War Memorial, Holy Trinity Church and village pubs nearby. The hall is bright and warm, and Penny keeps each class a comfortable size — never crammed.

Getting there

  • By car: free onsite parking at the village hall (see the LGVA venue page for details).
  • By bus: several local bus routes serve Leverstock Green within a short walk of the hall; check Intalink for current timetables.

What to bring

  • A yoga mat.
  • Loose, comfortable clothing you can move in.
  • A blanket if you have one — useful for the relaxation at the end.

Pricing and booking

Classes are £10 per session and are booked as a block (typically one term at a time). If you are new to the class, Penny offers a one-off trial session so you can try a class before committing. Get in touch to arrange a trial or to ask about the next available block.

Book a Tuesday trial in Leverstock Green

Tell Penny which slot you'd prefer (12:30pm or 2:00pm) and she'll reply about trials and the next available block.
