Online Gentle Yoga via Zoom

A live, interactive gentle Hatha Yoga class held every Wednesday evening from 7:00pm to 8:15pm via Zoom. Practise from your own mat at home with BWY-qualified teacher Penny Cronyn. £9 per session.

The class

This is a real, live class — not a recording. Each session lasts 75 minutes and combines simple breathwork, mindful movement and gentle Hatha Yoga postures, finishing with a guided relaxation. Penny can see the class throughout and offers individual cues and modifications, just as in a face-to-face class. The atmosphere is warm and friendly.

How it works

  1. Get in touch. Send Penny an email or message to ask about joining the next block.
  2. Install Zoom. Download the free Zoom app on your laptop, tablet or phone before the first class.
  3. Receive the link. After booking, Penny sends the joining link by email each week.
  4. Set up your space. A clear patch of floor a little wider than your mat is plenty. A blanket nearby is useful for the relaxation.
  5. Click and join. Open the link a few minutes before 7:00pm and you're in.

What you need at home

  • A yoga mat and a clear space to lie down and stretch.
  • A device with a camera (laptop, tablet or phone) and a stable internet connection.
  • Loose, comfortable clothing.
  • A blanket for the closing relaxation. Optional: blocks, a strap, or a cushion.

Pets and small audiences welcome — Pablo set the standard.

Pricing and booking

Classes are £9 per session and are booked as a block, usually one term at a time. New students are very welcome to try a one-off session before committing. Just get in touch and Penny will send the joining details.
